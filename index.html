How it Works

It’s Easy to Use

  1. Choose your platform. Use the website or download the free Autos Today app.
  2. Add your vehicle. Follow the prompts to upload your vehicle information.
  3. Get bids and finalize your deal! Once your vehicle has been added, alerts are sent to pre-screened car dealerships nationwide. All messaging can be done securely and privately through the app.
